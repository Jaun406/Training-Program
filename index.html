Tips
Sleep
Sleep is your #1 recovery tool
Aim for 7–9 hours. With 5am starts, be in bed by 9–9:30pm. Growth hormone is released during deep sleep — skipping sleep kills gains.
Nutrition
Hit 150–160g protein every day
Eggs, chicken, tuna and oats are your foundation. Don't fear carbs — at your training volume you need them for energy and recovery.
Bench Progress
Add weight only when you earn it
Add 2.5 kg only when you hit the top of your rep range two sessions in a row. Log every session — progressive overload is the only path to 120 kg.
Arms
Triceps make up 2/3 of arm size
Don't skip the overhead tricep extension on Thursday — it stretches the long head, the largest head of the tricep and the one that adds the most mass.
Hormones
Protect your natural testosterone
Keep sessions under 60 min, eat enough dietary fat (eggs, chicken thighs, peanut butter), manage stress, and get morning sunlight within 30 min of waking.
Body Fat
You're at ~15–18% — target 10–12%
A 200–300 calorie deficit with high protein will lean you out without losing muscle. At 21, body recomp is very achievable — be patient and consistent.
Mind-Muscle
Feel every rep
Before each set, touch the muscle you're about to work. Consciously squeeze and contract it during every rep. This isn't pseudoscience — it measurably increases muscle activation.
